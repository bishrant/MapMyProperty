The Layers window allows display of one or more overlay layers:

Contours:  USGS Elevation Contours service from The National Map that displays contours generated for the United States at various scales.

Flood Zones:  This layer displays Flood Hazard Areas from the Flood Insurance Rate Map created by the Federal Emergency Management Agency.

Hydrology:  Cartographic representation of the National Hydrography Dataset (NHD).

Watersheds:  This layer contains Hydrologic Unit (HU) polygon boundaries for the United States, Puerto Rico, and the U.S. Virgin Islands.  For additional information visit https://hydro.nationalmap.gov/.

Wet Areas:  THis layer displays wet areas from the National Wetlands Inventory.

Parcels:  ParcelPoint® from CoreLogic ® captures parcel boundary information and covers all 50 states, the District of Columbia, Guam, U.S. Virgin Islands, and Northern Mariana Islands.
